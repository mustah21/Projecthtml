UI/UX Designer

A UI/UX developer is a specialized software developer who focuses on creating user interfaces for products, such as websites and mobile apps. They combine elements of graphic design, psychology, and engineering to create a product that looks good, easy and enjoyable to use.

Javascript Developer

A JavaScript developer creates and maintains online applications using the JavaScript programming language. They develop dynamic and interactive user interfaces, integrate business logic, and connect to back-end systems using HTML, CSS, and other technologies.

Back-end Developer

Back-end developers are the experts who build and maintain the mechanisms that process data and perform actions on websites. They control everything you can see on a website, and are involved in data storage, security, and other server-side functions that you cannot see.

Full stack developer

A full-stack developer is a developer or an engineer who can build both the front end and the back end of a website or of an android or IOS. They are proficient in Key languages necessary for development such as JavaScript, Python, Ruby, and Java.
